# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

## Overview

Grocery List ("the App") is a household grocery list management application. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from your identity provider. We use this to create and identify your account.

### Grocery Data
We store the grocery lists, items, and shopping trips you create. This data is shared with members of your household.

### Usage Data
We collect anonymized telemetry data (screen views, API response times) to monitor app performance and reliability. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

## How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the service**: Your account and grocery data are used solely to operate the app and sync lists across your household.
- **Item categorization**: Item names you add may be sent to an AI service (Anthropic Claude) for automatic categorization. Only the item name is sent — no personal information.
- **Performance monitoring**: Anonymized telemetry helps us identify and fix bugs.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Your data is shared only with:
- **Your household members**: Other users in your household can see shared lists, items, and shopping trips.
- **Infrastructure providers**: AWS (hosting), Anthropic (item categorization AI). These providers process data only as needed to deliver their services.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Security

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in a secured PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS. Authentication is handled by AWS Cognito.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
